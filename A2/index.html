Terrorism in Asia Pacific

Most of the terrorist attacks were clustered in Asia.

Terrorist activities are more frequent in Asia than in Oceania.

India, Pakistan, Afghanistan and Philippines have a high frequency of terrorist attacks, so travel with caution to these areas.

Japan, China, and Australia are relatively safe, and terrorist attacks are rare.

Since 1986, the number of terrorist attacks with mass casualties has increased. In 1995, the number of casualties caused by terrorist attacks exceeded 10,000, and then declined slightly.

It began to grow again gradually in 2003 and reached an all-time high in 2015.
